About

This website was created as part of the PSE (Praxis in der Softwareentwicklung) course at KIT.

The goal is to promote and simplify the exchange of information regarding Open-Source traffic models in the area of traffic simulation and traffic management. The website is a platform for users to share their knowledge and experience with different traffic models.
